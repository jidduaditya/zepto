# Strategy Memo: Sing for Your Scoop

## The Insight

Blinkit owns the scream. Loud, chaotic, binary: you scream, you get ice cream. It is a gimmick that works once.

Zepto should not copy it. Zepto should counter it.

Screaming is noise. Singing is signal. Blinkit made ordering feel like a dare. We make it feel like a game.

## The Mechanic

**Sing for Your Scoop.** The app plays a two-note melody for "ice cream." The user sings it back. The app scores how close their pitch was. The score unlocks a graded discount:

| Score | Tier | Discount |
|-------|------|----------|
| 90-100 | Pitch Perfect | 20% off |
| 70-89 | Sweet Voice | 15% off |
| 50-69 | Nice Try | 10% off |
| 0-49 | Keep Singing | 5% off |

Everyone gets something. Nobody loses. But the top scorers get bragging rights and bigger discounts. That is the share trigger.

## Why This Is Uniquely Zepto

1. **Precision, not chaos.** Zepto's brand promise is speed with control: the right item, perfectly delivered, in 10 minutes. A graded melody score reinforces that identity. Blinkit's scream is a volume threshold. Ours is a skill game. The brand statement: "We don't just deliver fast. We deliver with taste."

2. **The 10-minute window.** The brief calls this the canvas. This mechanic lives at the start of the order: you sing, you earn, you buy. The 10-minute SLA delivers the reward. We are not competing on the input moment (scream vs sing). We are wrapping the entire order in a performance.

3. **Reusable.** The mechanic is category-agnostic. "Sing for Your Scoop" becomes "Sing for Chocolate" or "Hum for Biscuits" with a config change. The reference melody, the product tie-in, and the discount tiers are all parameterized. One build, infinite campaigns.

## Why Users Share

The share card is designed to provoke competition: "I sang for my scoop. Can you beat 87?" It works because:
- Scores are personal. Two friends will get different numbers.
- The grading creates stakes. "Sweet Voice" vs "Pitch Perfect" is a status game.
- The card is visually distinct. Purple-to-pink gradient, bold score, Zepto branding. Unmistakable in a feed.

This is not a coupon. It is a score. People share scores. They do not share coupons.

## What This Hits

1. **Screenshot = Zepto.** The gradient card, the score, the "Sing for Your Scoop" header. No logo needed.
2. **Organic mentions.** Singing into your phone is inherently a video moment. Reels, stories, X posts.
3. **15%+ ice cream lift.** Every discount drives a conversion. Graded discounts make users try multiple times.
4. **Win back Blinkit users.** "They made you scream. We let you sing." Counter-positioning in one line.
5. **Reusable.** Config-driven. Any impulse category. Any melody.

## One Sentence

Blinkit made ordering loud. We make it melodic.
